Notes on Rainfall Probabilities

Precipitation probabilities are provided by the National Weather Service's National Digital Forecast Database (NDFD). The probability values presented are the likelihood, expressed as a percent, of a measurable precipitation event (1/100th of an inch or more) at a grid point during the 12-hour valid period. The "night" probability covers the 12-hour period from 8pm the previous calendar day to 8am on the date listed. The "day" probability covers the 12-hour period from 8am to 8pm on the date indicated.
